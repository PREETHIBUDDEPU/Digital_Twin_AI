Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you use Digital Twin AI:

  • Account data – username, email, password hash (securely stored).
  • Financial data – income, expenses, savings, transactions you choose to log.
  • Study data – study hours, subjects, productivity scores.
  • Fitness data – activity types, duration, calories burned.
  • Goals – descriptions and target dates.
  • Chat messages – questions and responses with the AI assistant.
How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide personalised analytics, forecasts, and recommendations.
  • Enable the AI assistant to give context aware advice.
  • Track your progress and display dashboards.
  • Improve the platform (aggregated, anonymised insights).
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
Data Security

We use industry standard encryption (HTTPS) and secure password hashing (Werkzeug) to protect your data. All databases are stored securely and access is restricted to authorised administrators.

Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and to maintain your preferences. No third party tracking cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work as expected.

Your Rights
  • Access – view your personal data at any time.
  • Correct – update your profile information.
  • Delete – request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Object – restrict processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Third Party Services

We use Google Gemini for the AI assistant (optional). When you use the assistant, your questions and profile context may be sent to Google's API. No other third party services are used.

You can choose not to use the AI assistant if you prefer to avoid sharing data with third parties.
